Hamid Naderi Yeganeh

Mathematical Artist & Creator of Polar Function Art

Hamid Naderi Yeganeh is an Iranian mathematician and artist who creates stunning digital artwork using complex mathematical formulas, particularly polar functions with sine and cosine operations. His work involves drawing thousands of circles of varying sizes along looping paths, creating intricate and beautiful patterns that have been featured in major publications worldwide.
